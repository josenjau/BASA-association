About BASA

BASA is the official student association for Business Administration students at the College of Business Education (CBE). We are dedicated to enhancing the academic and professional development of our members through various programs, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Career Paths in Business Administration

Discover the diverse career opportunities available to Business Administration graduates. Our program prepares you for success in various industries and roles.

Management Roles

Business Manager

Oversee operations, manage teams, and drive business growth

  • Key Skills: Leadership, Strategic Planning, Decision Making

Operations Manager

Optimize business processes and improve efficiency

  • Key Skills: Process Optimization, Quality Control, Resource Management

Finance & Accounting

Financial Analyst

Analyze financial data and provide strategic recommendations

  • Key Skills: Financial Analysis, Data Interpretation, Risk Assessment

Account Manager

Manage client accounts and financial relationships

  • Key Skills: Client Relations, Financial Planning, Communication

Marketing & Sales

Marketing Manager

Develop and implement marketing strategies

  • Key Skills: Digital Marketing, Market Research, Brand Management

Sales Manager

Lead sales teams and drive revenue growth

  • Key Skills: Sales Strategy, Customer Relations, Negotiation

Consulting & Advisory

Business Consultant

Provide expert advice to improve business performance

  • Key Skills: Problem Solving, Business Analysis, Communication

Project Manager

Lead and manage business projects

  • Key Skills: Project Planning, Team Management, Risk Management
